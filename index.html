Our Leadership

Pastors Rusty &
Lyudmila Fletcher

A Fountain of Life Worship Center is led by Pastors Rusty and Lyudmila Fletcher. Their heart is to see people transformed by the power of God's Word and to build a church dedicated to authentic discipleship.

"Our vision is to reach our community with the life-giving message of Jesus."

Based in Kyle, Texas, they have cultivated a community where every person is encouraged to grow in their faith and experience the living water of Christ.

Ministries

Ages 0 - 3

Nursery

Nursery care is provided each Sunday morning during both services and on Wednesday evenings. Age-appropriate toys, games, and lessons are provided for toddlers in a safe, loving environment.

"I praise you because I am fearfully and wonderfully made..." — Psalm 139:1

Ages 4 - 11

Children

Our leaders work hard to make the children's ministry educational and fun. Classes are held during the 11:15 am service, featuring Bible lessons, crafts, games, and music.

"All your children will be taught by the LORD..." — Isaiah 54:13

Ages 12 - 18

Youth

Youth gatherings are each Friday from 6:30 – 9:00 pm. The youth enjoy social activities, worship, teaching, and fellowship. Monthly off-site events include bowling, sports, and more.

"For I know the plans I have for you..." — Jeremiah 29:11

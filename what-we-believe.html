What We Believe

Our Tenets of Faith

"Establishing a foundation on the uncompromised Word of God."

01

The Holy Bible

WE BELIEVE … the entire Bible, Genesis through Revelation, is God's Word.

02

The One True God

WE BELIEVE … that there is one God, creator of all things, who eternally exists as the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

03

The Nature of Man

WE BELIEVE … that all people on earth have sinned and are, because of their sins, separated from God.

04

Jesus Christ

WE BELIEVE … that Jesus Christ is the Son of God the Father. He was begotten by the Holy Spirit and He was born of the virgin Mary. He came to restore our relationship with His Father.

05

The Resurrection

WE BELIEVE … that Jesus Christ died on the cross for the sins of the world, rose from the dead, and ascended to heaven to be with His Father.

06

Salvation

WE BELIEVE … that our Heavenly Father offers forgiveness of our sins and the gift of eternal life to everyone that believes in Jesus Christ and makes Him their Lord.

07

Water Baptism

WE BELIEVE … in water baptism by immersion as commanded by Jesus Christ and practiced by His disciples in the New Testament.

08

Eternal Life

WE BELIEVE … in the resurrection of the dead, the eternal happiness of the saved, and the eternal punishment of the lost.

09

The Holy Spirit

WE BELIEVE … in the baptism of the Holy Spirit as taught in the New Testament, experienced by the Apostles, and received by many Christian believers in the Book of Acts.

10

Divine Healing

WE BELIEVE … in God's power to physically heal our bodies through prayer and faith in Jesus Christ.

11

Spiritual Growth

WE BELIEVE … that it is our Heavenly Father's desire for all believers in Christ, to grow spiritually, prosper, and be blessed.

12

The Second Coming

WE BELIEVE … that Jesus Christ will one day physically and literally return to this Earth, and He will rule over all people here on Earth for 1,000 years.
